About

Bio

I'm a graphic designer and social media manager currently studying AI & Machine Learning at Ramdeobaba University, Nagpur. It's an unusual mix, but it means I design with both a visual eye and a strategist's sense of what actually gets attention.

Right now, I design campaign creatives and manage social channels for REEF (Ramdeobaba Engineers Environmental Forum), lead brand and content strategy as a Marketing Intern at CampusSwap, and co-founded The Day Off Club, a community initiative around intentional rest and digital well-being. For The Day Off Club, I handle everything from brand identity to the website and events.

Outside client and community work, I hold a senior diploma in Bharatnatyam and coordinate my university's Dance Club. I like design that has rhythm to it, whether it's on stage or on a page.
